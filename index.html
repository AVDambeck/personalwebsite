Kitkat Dambeck

Welcome to my personal website! My name is Kitkat and I am a genderqueer artist currently studying at the Mason Gross School of the Arts. I have a particular interest in print media, particularly in the creation of zines. In the past I've done work in photography and video. On my website, you can find more information about my art, my journey as a genderqueer artist, and my thoughts on various topics. Thank you for visiting and I hope you enjoy your stay.
